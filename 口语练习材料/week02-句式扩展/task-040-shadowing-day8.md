# Task 40: Shadowing Day 8 - One Sentence 10 Times

## 📚 PRE-STUDY [10 minutes]

### Before You Begin

**Welcome to a Different Shadowing Technique!**

Previous days: Shadow longer segments (1-3 minutes continuously)
Today: **Deep practice** on ONE sentence, repeated 10 times

### Key Concepts

**Why One Sentence 10 Times?**

| Approach | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| **Continuous shadowing** | Builds flow, stamina, overall rhythm |
| **Repetition practice** | Perfects specific sounds, intonation, stress |

**This technique is perfect for:**
- Problematic sounds (th, r/l, specific vowels)
- Intonation patterns (questions vs. statements)
- Word stress in multi-syllable words
- Challenging sentence structures

**The 10x Repetition Process:**
1. First 3 times: Focus on accuracy
2. Next 3 times: Focus on rhythm and stress
3. Next 3 times: Focus on natural delivery
4. Final time: Just speak naturally

#### STEP 3: The 10x Practice [12 min]

**Repetitions 1-3: Accuracy Focus**
- Listen to the original
- Speak along slowly
- Focus on getting every sound right
- Don't worry about naturalness yet

**Repetitions 4-6: Rhythm & Stress Focus**
- Focus on word stress
- Match the speaker's rhythm
- Pay attention to pauses
- Tap the stressed syllables

**Repetitions 7-9: Natural Delivery Focus**
- Speed up to normal pace
- Focus on emotion and expression
- Make it sound like YOUR speech
- Don't overthink

**Repetition 10: Final Run**
- Speak naturally without the audio
- Record yourself
- Compare with original

## 💡 COACH'S NOTES

### 🎓 Teaching Points

**1. Why Deep Practice Works**

Deep practice on ONE sentence:
- **Builds muscle memory** for specific patterns
- **Allows focused attention** on problem areas
- **Creates mastery** that transfers to other sentences
- **Is more efficient** than shallow practice on many sentences

**2. The Progression (Reps 1-10)**

| Phase | Focus | Mental State |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| 1-3 | Accuracy | Conscious, careful, slow |
| 4-6 | Rhythm | Less conscious, more flowing |
| 7-9 | Naturalness | Automatic, comfortable |
| 10 | Integration | Natural, personal |

**By Rep 10, the sentence should feel like YOUR speech, not an imitation!**

**3. Selecting Good Sentences**

Choose sentences with:
- **Interesting vocabulary** (learn words while practicing)
- **Complex structures** (relative clauses, conditionals, contrasts)
- **Clear emotion** (not monotone delivery)
- **Appropriate length** (10-20 words is ideal)

### 🔧 Common Pitfalls & Fixes

| Pitfall | Example | Coach's Fix |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Choosing too simple a sentence** | "I like apples." | Pick something with challenging sounds/structures |
| **Rushing the 10 reps** | Doing all reps in 2 minutes | Spend quality time on each repetition |
| **Not changing focus between reps** | Same approach every time | Use the 4-phase approach |
| **Never recording final attempt** | No comparison possible | Always record Rep 10 for comparison |
| **Choosing too long a sentence** | 40+ words, gets overwhelming | Keep to 10-20 words |
| **Only practicing easy sentences** | Avoiding challenges | Push yourself with difficult sounds! |

### 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Choose ONE problem area** per session (th sounds, word stress, etc.)
2. **Mark your transcript** with stress marks, pauses, intonation
3. **Listen before speaking**—hear it in your mind first
4. **Rep 10 should feel easy**—if not, do a few more
5. **Save your best sentence**—record it for comparison next week

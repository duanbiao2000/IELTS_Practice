### ELSA Score to IELTS Band Correlation

| ELSA Score | Approximate IELTS Pronunciation Band |
|------------|--------------------------------------|
| 90-100 | Band 8.0-9.0 (Excellent) |
| 80-89 | Band 7.0-7.5 (Very Good) |
| 70-79 | Band 6.0-6.5 (Good) |
| 60-69 | Band 5.0-5.5 (Modest) |
| Below 60 | Band 4.0-4.5 (Limited) |

**Note:** This is approximate. IELTS pronunciation includes intonation, rhythm, and stress beyond what ELSA measures.

## рҹ’Ў COACH'S NOTES

### рҹҺ“ Teaching Points

**1. Numbers Don't Lie**
> Your ELSA score is objective data. The improvement you see is REAL. You earned every point through deliberate practice.

**2. Perfection is Not the Goal**
A score of 85+ is excellent for IELTS Band 7+. You don't need 95+. Focus your remaining energy on fluency, vocabulary, and grammar.

**3. This is a Milestone, Not a Destination**
Your pronunciation will continue to improve after IELTS. This assessment is a checkpoint, not a final destination.

### рҹ”§ Common Pitfalls & Fixes

| Pitfall | Coach's Fix |
|---------|-------------|
| **Disappointed by score** | Compare with YOUR Week 1, not with perfection |
| **Obsessing over remaining problems** | Focus on how FAR you've come, not how far you have to go |
| **Thinking progress is too small** | Any positive improvement is real progress. Celebrate it. |
| **Forgetting to screenshot** | Screenshot NOW. You'll want this for your records. |
| **Not trusting the results** | ELSA's AI is consistent. If Week 1 was accurate, Week 4 is too. |

### рҹҺҜ Pro Tips

1. **Save both screenshots** вҖ“ Week 1 and Week 4 side by side
2. **Share your progress** вҖ“ post your improvement (it's motivating!)
3. **Set post-IELTS goals** вҖ“ what sounds will you tackle next?
4. **Keep using ELSA** вҖ“ even 5 minutes a day maintains progress

## ✍️ Key Sentence Structures (Writing)

### Task 1: Chart Description

1. **Introduction Pattern:**
   > "The bar chart illustrates [what] across [categories] in [context]."

2. **Overview Pattern:**
   > "Overall, [main trend], whereas [contrast]. Notably, [specific observation]."

3. **Comparison Pattern:**
   > "[Country] led with [percentage], followed closely by [Country] ([percentage]). [Country] lagged slightly behind at [percentage]."

4. **Trend Description:**
   > "A dramatic [increase/decrease] became evident. [Country] maintained relatively high rates, while [Country] showed substantial drops."

### Task 2: Argumentative Essay

1. **Thesis Statement:**
   > "While some argue that [viewpoint], I contend that [opposite viewpoint] because [reason 1] and [reason 2]."

2. **Topic Sentence:**
   > "The primary justification for [position] is [main reason]."

3. **Supporting Evidence:**
   > "For instance, [example]. This demonstrates that [connection to argument]."

4. **Counterargument:**
   > "Admittedly, [opposing view]. However, this overlooks [why it's wrong]."

5. **Conclusion:**
   > "In conclusion, I firmly believe that [restated position]. Therefore, [recommendation/future outlook]."

## 🎯 Useful Collocations (Writing)

### Academic Writing
- **exhibit high levels** (显示高水平)
- **demonstrate significant growth** (展示显著增长)
- **maintain a dominant position** (保持主导地位)
- **show a substantial decline** (显示大幅下降)
- **achieve moderate penetration** (达到中等普及率)

### Comparative Language
- **comparable figures** (可比的数据)
- **striking disparity** (显著差异)
- **inverse correlation** (负相关)
- **notable exception** (显著的例外)
- **consistent pattern** (一致的模式)

### Argumentative Phrases
- **contend that** (主张)
- **overlook the significance of** (忽视...的重要性)
- **provide a solid foundation** (提供坚实基础)
- **equip graduates with** (为毕业生配备)
- **strike a balance** (取得平衡)

## 💡 Key Insights (Today's Practice)

### Task 1 Success Factors:
1. **Overview First** - Identify main patterns without specific data
2. **Precise Data** - Report numbers accurately with comparative language
3. **Complex Sentences** - Use compound-complex structures for Band 7.5+
4. **Vocabulary Range** - Employ formal, academic terms (exhibit, demonstrate, whereas)

### Task 2 Success Factors:
1. **Clear Position** - State your opinion in introduction and conclusion
2. **Paragraph Structure** - One main idea per paragraph with topic sentences
3. **Counterarguments** - Acknowledge opposing views then refute them
4. **Specific Examples** - Provide concrete evidence to support claims

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- ❌ Listing all data without identifying trends
- ❌ Using simple sentences throughout Task 1
- ❌ Being vague in Task 2 (e.g., "important" instead of "essential for career advancement")
- ❌ Failing to compare different age groups/countries in Task 1
- ❌ Forgetting to restate your position in Task 2 conclusion

## 🎓 Key Learning Points

### Task 1 (Line Graph) - Band 7.5+ Techniques

**Structure:**
- Overview in separate paragraph after introduction
- Chronological organization (2000 → 2010 → 2020)
- Precise data reporting with markers ("approximately 45%", "around 92%")

**Vocabulary:**
- "provides a clear comparison of" (paraphrasing)
- "significant upward trend" (trend analysis)
- "dramatic surge" (descriptive verbs)
- "closed the gap substantially" (comparison)
- "near-universal access" (insightful conclusion)

**Insights:**
- Accurate paraphrasing without changing meaning
- Clear overview of main trends (highest, gaps, narrowing)
- Appropriate use of comparison adjectives ("highest", "lowest")

### Task 2 (Agree/Disagree) - Band 7.5+ Techniques

**Structure:**
- Clear thesis with nuanced position ("largely disagree")
- 3 body paragraphs: economic, social, counter-argument
- Conclusion restates thesis without new info

**Vocabulary:**
- "irreplaceable advantages" (sophisticated)
- "spontaneous collaborations" (academic)
- "knowledge spillovers" (industry-specific)
- "serendipitous encounters" (high-level)
- "symbiotic relationship" (complex)
- "deeply ingrained" (expressive)

**Insights:**
- Acknowledgment of counter-arguments shows critical thinking
- Specific examples (Silicon Valley) strengthen arguments
- Avoids overgeneralizations and absolutes
- Human/social dimensions add depth to argument

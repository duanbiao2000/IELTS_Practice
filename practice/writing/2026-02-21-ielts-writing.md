## 💡 Writing Tips for Band 7.5+

### Task 1 Tips

1. **Start with a clear overview** – Identify the main trends immediately after the introduction
2. **Use precise language** – Instead of "increased," use "surged," "climbed," "rose sharply"
3. **Group information logically** – Don't just list data; group similar trends or contrasting patterns
4. **Vary sentence structures** – Mix simple, compound, and complex sentences
5. **Include specific figures** – Mention actual numbers to demonstrate attention to detail
6. **Use accurate verbs for trends** – "Fluctuated," "stabilized," "plateaued," "plummeted"

### Task 2 Tips

1. **Clear thesis statement** – State your position clearly in the introduction
2. **Topic sentences** – Start each paragraph with a clear main idea
3. **Support with examples** – Provide specific examples, not just general statements
4. **Use cohesive devices** – Connect ideas with "Furthermore," "Additionally," "In contrast"
5. **Academic vocabulary** – Use formal language appropriate for academic writing
6. **Address all parts** – Ensure you fully answer both aspects of two-part questions

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Task 1:**
- Including personal opinions or interpretations not supported by data
- Describing every single data point without summarizing
- Using the same words repeatedly (e.g., "increase," "decrease")
- Missing the overview or placing it at the end

❌ **Task 2:**
- Straying from the topic
- Not fully answering both parts of the question
- Using informal language or slang
- Not providing sufficient examples or explanations
- Writing too little or too much (aim for 250-270 words)

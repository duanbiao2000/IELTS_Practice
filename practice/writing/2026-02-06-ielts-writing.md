# IELTS Writing Practice - 2026-02-06

## Task 1: Graph Description (柱状图)

### 题目
**The chart below shows the percentage of commuters using different modes of transport in a European city between 1990 and 2020. Summarize the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.**

假设数据：
- 1990年：Public transport 45%, Car 30%, Bicycle 15%, Walking 10%
- 2000年：Public transport 40%, Car 40%, Bicycle 12%, Walking 8%
- 2010年：Public transport 35%, Car 45%, Bicycle 12%, Walking 8%
- 2020年：Public transport 30%, Car 50%, Bicycle 10%, Walking 10%

---

### Model Answer (Band 7.5+)

**[Introduction]** The bar chart illustrates the changing trends in transport preferences among commuters in a European city over a 30-year period from 1990 to 2020. Four modes of transport are compared: public transport, private cars, bicycles, and walking.

> **【注解1】开头句标准模板**
> - "illustrates" 替代重复使用的 "shows"
> - "changing trends in transport preferences" 概括图表主题
> - "over a 30-year period" 明确时间跨度
> - 四种交通方式作为引出，为下文铺垫

**[Overview]** Overall, the most striking feature is the dramatic rise in car usage, which more than doubled during the period, while public transport experienced a steady decline. Bicycle and walking both remained relatively marginal, with only slight fluctuations observed.

> **【注解2】总体段落**
> - "most striking feature" 强调最显著变化
> - "more than doubled" 精确描述增长倍数
> - "steady decline" 描述下降趋势
> - "marginal" 指次要的、不重要的
> - "fluctuations" 指波动变化

**[Body 1]** In 1990, public transport was the dominant choice, accounting for nearly half (45%) of all commuters. However, this figure experienced a consistent downward trajectory, falling to 40% in 2000, then to 35% in 2010, and finally reaching 30% by 2020—a decline of 15 percentage points over three decades.

> **【注解3】主体段落1 - 主要趋势描述**
> - "dominant choice" 描述主导地位
> - "accounting for nearly half" 使用占百分比的表达
> - "consistent downward trajectory" 描述持续下降（轨迹）
> - 精确列出各年数据点，增强准确性
> - "decline of 15 percentage points" 总结总变化量

**[Body 2]** Conversely, private cars witnessed the most dramatic surge. Starting at 30% in 1990, car usage increased steadily to match public transport in 2000 (40%), then overtook it in subsequent years. By 2020, exactly half of all commuters were driving to work, representing a 67% increase from its initial level.

> **【注解4】主体段落2 - 对比趋势**
> - "Conversely" 表示相反对比（连接词）
> - "witnessed the most dramatic surge" 描述最显著激增
> - "match", "overtake" 描述数值变化关系
> - "representing a 67% increase" 计算并总结增长率
> - 数字精确表达

**[Body 3]** The remaining two modes of transport—bicycles and walking—maintained relatively low usage throughout the period. Bicycle usage fluctuated slightly between 10% and 15%, while walking remained stable at around 8-10%, with a marginal increase observed in 2020.

> **【注解5】主体段落3 - 次要信息**
> - "maintained relatively low usage" 描述稳定低位
> - "fluctuated slightly" 描述轻微波动
> - "stable at around" 描述稳定状态
> - "marginal increase" 描述微小增长
> - 使用破折号补充说明

## Task 2: Essay Discussion (讨论双方观点)

### 题目
**Some people believe that technology has brought people closer together, while others argue that it has actually increased social isolation. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.**

---

### Model Answer (Band 7.5+)

**[Introduction]** The impact of technology on human relationships has sparked intense debate in recent years. While some individuals contend that digital innovations have bridged distances and strengthened connections, others maintain that these very same technologies have paradoxically alienated people from one another. This essay will examine both perspectives before presenting my own viewpoint.

> **【注解6】引言段 - 标准结构**
> - "sparked intense debate" 引起激烈争论
> - "contend that" 论证/认为（替换argue）
> - "bridged distances" 缩短距离
> - "paradoxically alienated" 矛盾地疏远
> - "examining both perspectives" 预示文章结构

**[Body 1 - View 1: Technology brings people closer]** Proponents of the first perspective emphasize how technology has revolutionized communication. Social media platforms, video calling applications, and instant messaging services have enabled individuals to maintain relationships across vast geographical barriers. For instance, families separated by oceans can now see each other daily through FaceTime or Skype, something unimaginable just two decades ago. Furthermore, online communities have emerged where people with shared interests—be it hobbies, professional goals, or health challenges—can connect and support one another, transcending physical limitations.

> **【注解7】主体段1 - 第一个观点**
> - "Proponents of the first perspective" 观点支持者
> - "revolutionized communication" 革命性改变沟通
> - "vast geographical barriers" 广泛的地理障碍
> - "unimaginable just two decades ago" 时间对比
> - "emerged where people with shared interests" 定语从句
> - "transcending physical limitations" 超越物理限制

**[Body 2 - View 2: Technology increases isolation]** On the other hand, critics argue that technology has created what sociologists term "digital alienation." Excessive screen time has been linked to reduced face-to-face interactions, with people preferring to communicate through devices rather than meeting in person. This phenomenon is particularly evident among younger generations, who often choose to socialize virtually even when physically together. Moreover, social media platforms can foster feelings of inadequacy and loneliness, as individuals constantly compare themselves with curated online personas, leading to what psychologists call "social comparison anxiety."

> **【注解8】主体段2 - 第二个观点**
> - "critics argue" 批评者认为（与前段proponents对比）
> - "digital alienation" 数字化疏离（术语）
> - "Excessive screen time" 过度屏幕时间
> - "particularly evident among younger generations" 特定群体
> - "socialize virtually" 虚拟社交
> - "curated online personas" 精心策划的在线人设
> - "social comparison anxiety" 社交比较焦虑（术语）

**[Body 3 - Personal opinion]** In my view, technology is a double-edged sword that reflects rather than determines the quality of human relationships. The key lies in how individuals choose to utilize these tools. When technology serves as a means to enhance existing relationships—for instance, by keeping families connected across distances—it undoubtedly brings people closer. However, when it becomes a substitute for genuine human interaction, replacing meaningful conversations with superficial online exchanges, it inevitably contributes to social isolation. Therefore, the impact of technology on social cohesion depends largely on individual intentionality and self-discipline.

> **【注解9】个人观点段 - 深度分析**
> - "double-edged sword" 双刃剑（经典比喻）
> - "reflects rather than determines" 反映而非决定（精辟观点）
> - "serves as a means to enhance" 作为增强的手段
> - "==substitute for genuine human interaction==" 替代真实人际互动
> - "superficial online exchanges" 表面的在线交流
> - "inevitably contributes to" 不可避免地导致
> - "depends largely on individual intentionality" 取决于个人意图

**[Conclusion]** To conclude, while technology has undeniably expanded our capacity to connect with others globally, it has also introduced new forms of social disconnection. Ultimately, whether technology brings people together or drives them apart depends on how mindfully and responsibly we choose to integrate it into our lives. A balanced approach that leverages digital tools without sacrificing face-to-face relationships represents the optimal path forward.

> **【注解10】结论段 - 总结与升华**
> - "undeniably expanded" 不可否认地扩展
> - "introduced new forms of social disconnection" 引入新形式的社交断连
> - "mindfully and responsibly" 审慎和负责任地（副词并列）
> - "A balanced approach that leverages" 一种平衡的方法利用
> - "without sacrificing" 不牺牲
> - "optimal path forward" 最佳前进道路

## 词汇与表达亮点总结

- **程度副词**: consistently, steadily, dramatically, marginally, relatively
- **趋势描述**: downward trajectory, upward trend, consistent decline, dramatic surge
- **百分比表达**: accounting for, representing, percentage points, half, double

### Task 2 高频词汇
- **论证动词**: contend, maintain, emphasize, argue, present
- **学术术语**: digital alienation, social comparison anxiety, curated online personas
- **连接词**: Conversely, Furthermore, Moreover, In my view, Therefore
- **深度表达**: double-edged sword, transcend physical limitations, serves as a means, inevitably contributes to

1. **复合句**: While some individuals contend..., others maintain...
2. **分词短语**: families separated by oceans..., people preferring to communicate...
3. **定语从句**: communities where people with shared interests can connect...
4. **插入语**: something unimaginable just two decades ago
5. **强调结构**: The key lies in how individuals choose to utilize these tools.

## 写作建议

### Task 1 提分要点
1. **开头段**: 1-2句，概括图表主题、时间、对象
2. **总体段**: 2-3句，总结最显著趋势
3. **主体段**: 2-3段，按逻辑分组描述数据
4. **避免逐句描述**: 不要机械地逐一描述每个数据点
5. **使用对比**: 显性比较不同类别的变化

### Task 2 提分要点
1. **明确立场**: 题目要求"discuss both views"时必须平衡论述
2. **层次分明**: 观点1→观点2→个人观点，逻辑清晰
3. **具体例子**: 每个观点后跟上具体的现实例子
4. **深度分析**: 不要停留在表面，要分析原因和影响
5. **词汇升级**: 使用更精确、更高级的词汇替换基础词汇

### 常见错误避免
1. ❌ 任务回应不全：只论述一方观点
2. ❌ 结构混乱：没有清晰的段落划分
3. ❌ 词汇重复：过度使用同一个词
4. ❌ 语法错误：主谓不一致、时态混乱
5. ❌ 字数不达标：Task 1低于150词，Task 2低于250词

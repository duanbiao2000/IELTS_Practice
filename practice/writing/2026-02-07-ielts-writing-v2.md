**题目：** Coffee consumption in five countries (cups per day, 2023)

The bar chart illustrates the average daily coffee consumption among adults in five different countries in 2023, measured in cups per person. Overall, Finland exhibited the highest consumption level, while Japan recorded the lowest figure. The Nordic countries showed a clear preference for coffee, with all nations consuming more than three cups daily.

Notably, four out of five countries exceeded the global average of 2.2 cups per day, suggesting that these nations have particularly strong coffee cultures. The data reveals a geographical clustering, as the top three countries are all situated in Northern Europe.

### 💡 Task 1 高分策略

#### ✅ 核心技巧

**1. Overview First - 总述优先**
- 总是包含清晰的 overview 语句
- 立即识别主要趋势、对比或异常值
- 可以放在第一段，也可以单独成段

**2. Vary Your Verbs - 动词多样化**
不要重复 "shows" 或 "is"，使用替代词：
- ✅ **illustrates, demonstrates, depicts, reveals, highlights, presents, indicates**
- ❌ shows, is, shows, is, shows...

**3. Precise Data Reporting - 精确数据报告**
不要只是列出数字，使用精确语言：
- ✅ "approximately 3.5 cups"
- ✅ "exhibited a marginal increase"
- ❌ "about 3.5"
- ❌ "went up a little"

**4. Group and Compare - 分组比较**
逻辑组织数据：
- 从高到低 / 从低到高
- 按相似点和不同点分组
- 按类别或分组

**5. Synthesize, Don't List - 综合而非罗列**
寻找模式，不是个别数据点：
- ✅ "The Nordic countries showed consistently higher consumption"
- ✅ "Japan represented an outlier with significantly lower figures"
- ❌ "Finland was 3.5, Sweden was 3.2, Netherlands was 2.8..."

#### ✅ 句型模板

**开头句（介绍）**
- The [chart type] illustrates [what it shows] in [time period].
- The [chart type] provides information about [topic] during [time period].

**总述句（Overview）**
- Overall, [highest] exhibited the highest [metric], while [lowest] recorded the lowest figure.
- The most noticeable trend is that [main trend].

**对比句**
- [Country A] led the rankings, with [data], followed closely by [Country B] at [data].
- In contrast, [Country X] demonstrated markedly lower [metric] at just [data].

**总结句**
- The disparity between [X] and [Y] was substantial, with [comparison].
- The data reveals a/an [pattern], as [explanation].

#### ✅ 常见错误

❌ **Task 1 绝对不要做的事：**
- 给出你的观点
- 解释为什么数据存在（不要过度解读）
- 抄题目中的词（改写！）
- 写太多细节而没有 overview

❌ **常见低分表达：**
- The chart shows that... → **The chart illustrates that...**
- The difference is big. → **The disparity is substantial.**
- Finland is the highest. → **Finland exhibited the highest level.**
- Japan is much lower. → **Japan demonstrated markedly lower consumption.**

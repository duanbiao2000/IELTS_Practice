## 📖 Reading Passage

### Passage Topic: The Impact of Social Media on Mental Health

Social media has become an integral part of modern life, with billions of people worldwide using platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok. While these platforms offer numerous benefits, including instant communication and access to information, growing evidence suggests that excessive social media use may have negative effects on mental health.

Research indicates that social media can contribute to feelings of anxiety and depression. The constant comparison with others' seemingly perfect lives can lead to feelings of inadequacy and low self-esteem. Studies have found that young adults who spend more than two hours per day on social media are more likely to report symptoms of depression compared to those who spend less time.

Another concern is the impact on sleep quality. The blue light emitted by electronic devices can disrupt the body's natural sleep-wake cycle, making it difficult to fall asleep. Furthermore, the fear of missing out (FOMO) often leads users to check their phones late at night, further compromising sleep duration and quality.

However, it is important to note that social media is not inherently harmful. When used mindfully, these platforms can provide social support, particularly for individuals who may feel isolated in their offline lives. Online communities can offer valuable resources and connections for people with shared interests or experiences.

Experts recommend taking a balanced approach to social media use. Setting time limits, taking regular breaks, and being selective about the content consumed can help mitigate negative effects. Additionally, practicing digital detox—periodically disconnecting from social media—can help restore mental well-being and improve real-life relationships.

Ultimately, the key lies in awareness and moderation. By understanding the potential risks and benefits, individuals can make informed choices about their social media habits and maintain a healthy digital lifestyle.

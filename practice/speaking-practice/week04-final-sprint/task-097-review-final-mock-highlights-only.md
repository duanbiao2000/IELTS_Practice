# Task 97: Review Final Mock – Highlights Only

## 📚 PRE-STUDY [5 minutes]

### Before You Begin

**Why focus ONLY on highlights?**

Your default mode is probably finding problems. Today:
- **Balance the scales** – you usually focus on what's wrong
- **Build confidence** – you need to believe in your abilities
- **See your progress** – acknowledge how far you've come
- **Enter exam positively** – confidence improves performance

### The Problem-Finding Bias

| Typical Review | Today's Review |
|----------------|----------------|
| What went wrong? | What went RIGHT? |
| What needs fixing? | What was EXCELLENT? |
| Why I struggled | How I SHINED |
| Problems, gaps, issues | Strengths, wins, successes |

## 💡 COACH'S NOTES

### 🎓 Teaching Points

**1. What You Focus On Expands**
> If you focus on problems, you'll see more problems. If you focus on strengths, you'll see more strengths. Your brain finds what you look for.

**2. Your Highlights Are Real**
The highlights you found today aren't "imaginary" or "exaggerated." They're REAL. You DID do those things well. Acknowledge them.

**3. Confidence is Legitimate**
Your confidence from this task isn't "fake" or "unfounded." It's based on EVIDENCE – your actual performance highlights.

### 🔧 Common Pitfalls & Fixes

| Pitfall | Coach's Fix |
|---------|-------------|
| **"But I still have problems!"** | Not today. Today is for strengths ONLY. Problems can wait. |
| **Feeling arrogant for celebrating** | This isn't arrogance – it's accurate self-assessment. You need balance. |
| **Downplaying successes** | "It wasn't THAT good" → Yes, it WAS. Accept the compliment. |
| **Comparing with perfection** | Compare with your Week 1 self, not with impossible perfection. |
| **Thinking this is unrealistic** | This is AS realistic as focusing only on problems. Both are partial truths. |

### 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Save your highlight reel** – read it before the real exam
2. **Share with someone** – say "These are my strengths" out loud
3. **Add to it daily** – find one more highlight each day until the exam
4. **Visualize your highlights** – imagine yourself performing this way in the real exam
5. **Believe your evidence** – your highlights are PROOF of capability

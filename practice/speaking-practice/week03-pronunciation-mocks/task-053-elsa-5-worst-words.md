### Before You Begin
**Why target problem words?**

Your mock exam recording revealed words you struggle with. These are your **personal pronunciation demons**. By identifying and systematically practicing your specific problem words, you:
- Eliminate recurring errors that lower your score
- Build confidence on words you actually use
- See measurable progress in one focused session

### The 20x Repetition Rule

Research on pronunciation training shows:
- **3-5 repetitions**: Familiarity
- **10 repetitions**: Improvement
- **20+ repetitions**: Muscle memory retention

## ЁЯТб COACH'S NOTES

### ЁЯОУ Teaching Points

**1. Why These 5 Words Matter**
These aren't random wordsтАФthey're words YOU actually use in your speaking. Fixing your personal high-frequency problem words gives you more improvement than fixing words you'll never say.

**2. The 20x Repetition Science**
- First 5 reps: Your brain is learning
- Next 10 reps: Your muscles are learning
- Final 5 reps: Building permanent memory
- Don't rushтАФquality over speed

**3. Track Your Progress**
If your score isn't improving after 10 attempts, STOP. Check:
- Are you listening to the model first?
- Can you hear the difference between your version and the model?
- Do you understand what sound you're targeting?

### ЁЯФз Common Pitfalls & Fixes

| Pitfall | Coach's Fix |
|---------|-------------|
| **Rushing through repetitions** | Set a timer. 20 reps ├Ч 5 words = 25 minutes minimum |
| **Not listening to the model first** | Always listen 2x before your first attempt |
| **Same score after 10 attempts** | You're practicing the error. Stop, listen to model again, restart |
| **Choosing words you never use** | Use words from your mock exam, not random difficult words |
| **Giving up after reaching 80%** | Push for 90%+. Muscle memory needs precision |

### ЁЯОп Pro Tips

1. **Record before and after**тАФsay all 5 words before practicing, then again after
2. **Note the specific sound** causing problems (e.g., "th" in "think")
3. **Practice in sentences** too, not just isolation
4. **Come back in 2 days**тАФtest retention. If score dropped, practice again

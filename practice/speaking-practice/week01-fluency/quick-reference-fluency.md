## 🎤 IPA Pronunciation Quick Reference

### Critical Sounds for Chinese Speakers

| Symbol | Sound | Common Words | Mouth Position |
|--------|-------|--------------|---------------|
| **/θ/** | voiceless th | think, thought, anything | Tongue BETWEEN teeth, blow air |
| **/ð/** | voiced th | this, that, those | Same position + VOICE (vibrate) |
| **/æ/** | short a | cat, man, that | Mouth WIDE open, tongue low |
| **/v/** | v sound | very, have, over | Top teeth on bottom lip |
| **/ʃ/** | sh sound | she, station, sure | Round lips, push air out |
| **/tʃ/** | ch sound | chair, match | Similar to Chinese 'q' |
| **/ŋ/** | ng sound | sing, thinking | Back of tongue UP, air through NOSE |

### Quick Self-Correction

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| "sink" instead of "think" | Stick tongue between teeth |
| "zis" instead of "this" | Tongue between teeth + vibrate voice |
| "men" instead of "man" | Open mouth WIDE like at dentist |
| "wery" instead of "very" | Bite bottom lip with top teeth |

## 🔗 Basic Connecting Words

### Simple & Natural

| Purpose | Words | Example |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Add ideas** | and, also, besides, too | "I like reading, **and** I enjoy writing." |
| **Show contrast** | but, however, although | "It's hard, **but** I keep practicing." |
| **Give reasons** | because, since, as | "I practice **because** I want to improve." |
| **Add examples** | for example, for instance, such as | "I love fruits, **such as** apples and bananas." |
| **Show sequence** | first, then, next, finally | "**First** I prepare, **then** I speak." |

### Quick Practice Template

```
"I like [topic], and I also enjoy [related topic]."
"[Statement], but [contrasting idea]."
"I [action] because [reason]."
"For example, [specific example]."
```

## 📋 Part 1 Question Types

### Common Categories & Quick Response Structure

| Category | Typical Questions | Response Formula |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| **Work/Studies** | What do you do? Why did you choose? | "I'm a [role/student]. I chose it because [reason]." |
| **Home** | Do you live in house/apartment? Favorite room? | "I live in a [type]. My favorite room is [room] because [reason]." |
| **Hobbies** | What do you do in free time? | "In my free time, I enjoy [activity]. It's [adjective] because..." |
| **Daily Routine** | Morning/evening routine? | "Usually I [action], then I [action]." |
| **Preferences** | Do you prefer X or Y? | "I prefer [option] because [reason]." |

### Quick Response Formula (3 parts)

```
1. Direct Answer: "Yes/No/I'm a..." OR "I prefer..."
2. Brief Reason: "...because..." OR "The main reason is..."
3. Small Detail: "For example..." OR "Especially..."
```

**Example**:
> "Do you prefer studying alone or with others?"
>
> "I prefer studying **alone** (1). The main reason is that I can **focus better** (2). For example, in a quiet room, I can concentrate **without distractions** (3)."

## 🎯 Shadowing Basics

### The Shadowing Process

1. **Listen once** - understand the content
2. **Read transcript** - notice patterns
3. **Shadow at 0.75x-0.85x speed** - mimic rhythm, stress, intonation
4. **Shadow at 1.0x** - normal speed
5. **Record yourself** - compare with original

### Key Focus Points

| Element | What to Listen For | How to Mimic |
|---------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Pausing** | Where speaker stops | Mark pauses with `/` in transcript |
| **Stress** | Which words get emphasis | Stress CONTENT words (nouns, verbs, adjectives) |
| **Intonation** | Pitch rise and fall | Questions rise, statements fall |
| **Linking** | How words connect | "turn it on" → "tur-ni-ton" |

## 🎼 Rhythm & Stress Patterns

### English is Stress-Timed (not syllable-timed)

**Correct**:
> "I **WANT** to **GO** to the **MAR**ket."
> (Stress on WANT, GO, MAR)

**Incorrect** (robotic):
> "I want to go to the mar-ket."
> (Equal stress on all syllables)

### Quick Stress Rule

1. **Content words** get stress: nouns, main verbs, adjectives, adverbs
2. **Function words** are weak: articles, prepositions, pronouns, auxiliary verbs

- [ ] /θ/ or /ð/: Briefly touch tongue to teeth
- [ ] /v/: Lightly bite bottom lip
- [ ] /r/: Pull tongue tip back
- [ ] /æ/: Drop jaw open wide
- [ ] /l/: Tap tongue behind teeth

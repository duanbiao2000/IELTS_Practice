**2. Foundation Is Built**
Week 1 was about foundation.
You now have:
- A baseline to measure from
- Frameworks to build on
- Habits to continue
- Skills to refine

**Week 2 will build on this foundation.**

**3. Progress Principle**
You might not feel like a "different speaker" yet.
That's okay. That's normal.

But look at your DATA:
- Fewer pauses
- More vocabulary
- Better structure
- More confidence

**This is progress. Trust the process. Continue the work.**

### 🔧 Common Pitfalls & Fixes

| Pitfall | Problem | Coach's Fix |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Not celebrating wins** | Demotivating, feels like endless work | Celebrate EVERY completion, every improvement |
| **Focusing only on weaknesses** | Misses how far you've come | Balance wins + areas for work |
| **Comparing to others** | Unfair, discouraging | Compare ONLY to your past self |
| **Expecting overnight transformation** | Unrealistic, leads to quitting | Language learning takes time—be patient |
| **Stopping now** | Losing momentum | You've built habits—keep going! |
